Privacy Policy - The Ledger

Effective Date: 01 November 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. The Ledger (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the personal information you share while using our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Only information you provide voluntarily such as your name and email when signing in.

Usage Data: Non-identifiable data like app crashes, screen views, and general app usage statistics.

Firebase Data: The Ledger uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Analytics. These services may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information
  • Crash logs
  • App interaction data
  • Authentication-related identifiers
This data is used solely to provide secure login, store your expense data safely, and improve app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

To provide and improve app functionality.

To secure your account using Firebase Authentication.

To store and sync your expense data using Cloud Firestore.

To analyze app usage and enhance the user experience through Firebase Analytics.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices and Firebase’s secure infrastructure to protect your data. However, no method of digital transmission is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy
