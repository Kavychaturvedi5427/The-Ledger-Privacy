Account Deletion Request – The Ledger

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from The Ledger, you can request account deletion using the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to the developer at:

reportthis.kavy.dev@gmail.com

Your email must include:

  • Your registered email address used in the app
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email & UID)
  • All expense entries stored in Cloud Firestore

What Data Is Not Deleted?

No data is retained after deletion. Everything linked to your account is permanently removed.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

Your account and data will be deleted within 7 days after the request is received.
